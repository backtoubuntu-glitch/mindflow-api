Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email.
Can I use MindFlow on multiple devices?
Yes! You can access MindFlow on any device with a web browser. Your progress syncs automatically across all devices, so you can start a lesson on your computer and continue on your tablet or phone.
How does Khensani AI personalize my learning?
Khensani AI analyzes your learning patterns, quiz results, and engagement to create personalized learning paths. It adapts difficulty levels, suggests relevant content, and provides targeted feedback to help you learn more effectively.
Is my child's data safe and private?
Absolutely. We take data privacy seriously and comply with all relevant regulations. Student data is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and used only to improve the learning experience. Parents have full control over data sharing settings.
How do the safety features work?
Our safety features include location tracking with geofencing, arrival/departure notifications, and emergency contact systems. Parents can set safe zones and receive alerts when their child enters or leaves these areas.
What subjects and grades are available?
MindFlow currently covers Grades 1-7 with subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, Chess, and Robotics. We're continuously adding new content and plan to expand to higher grades soon.
